It's a small world

The purpose of this experimental tool is to encourage us to listen to a wide range of opinions and to prevent the phenomenon of narrowing of vision, which is known as the "filter bubble".

The function is...

When you search, this tool will sometimes shows you the opposite words of the keyword you are searching for at the bottom of the search box.

*The maximum number of keywords is 8.This tool extracts one word from your search terms and shows you the opposite word(s) and the results page if you search using that word. Antonyms are retrieved in order from the front of the sentence.
